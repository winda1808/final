Jose Pet Clinic commissioned me to design their lobby backdrop artwork for Balikpapan's Anniversary. It has to integrate Borneo's endemic faunas, common pets, and Balikpapan's landmarks. I use Jose's color palette for the overall theme. How many animals can you find?

Jose Grooming
Illustrations

Jose Pet Clinic commissioned me to design their grooming service flyers and elevator artwork. The client wanted illustrations of a cat and a dog, but they wanted no personification, just a pet acting like a pet. I drew them in a bubbly bathtub to represent grooming and toys on their heads to make them seem innocent and cute. The cat's face looks grumpy because this cat hates bath time :)

Tshirt for "Gerakan Melingkar" Auction
Illustration

My concept is to design a merchandise t-shirt with a more designated graphic that looks colorful, fun, and youthful, as opposed to typical merchandise t-shirts that have animal photos printed on them. I drew Borneo endemic faunas and vegetation background with little detail to make it look childish. I volunteered this design for the Gerakan Melingkar auction, the profit was given to Orangutan Sanctuary.

Fiesta Condom Packaging Competition
Illustration

I drew this for the #MoretoDesign competition. I wanted to make condom packaging look youthful. Since sex seems taboo to talk about with our children, sex ed is hard to access. The youth ignore safety and become the victim of STDs and unwanted pregnancies. Many think contraception is a gateway to a free-sex lifestyle, so they don't talk about it. But contraceptions are not responsible for the choices people make. Instead, they can help to protect from the risk. The crocodile is a playful taunt on "Buaya Darat". Winners of the competition had their designs printed as packaging to collect and a chance for exposure. In fact, I got the photo from a person who contacted me via my Instagram username on the packaging.

Pulang Sekolah
Character Design

"Pulang Sekolah" or After School is portrayed as a child wearing an undershirt and Indonesian elementary school pants uniform. A representation of typical Indonesian kids as they arrive home from school, and can't wait to go out and play without even changing their clothes. He is also wearing a straw hat and holding a butterfly net, although he will mostly go and catch some frogs by the drain. The design is a copy of one of the boys in my "Summer Gang" illustration project. I created a character whom I think is close to heart, relatable, and interesting looking; even without fancy clothes. This character could be a mascot for kids' products or an illustration for elementary schools' collateral design.
